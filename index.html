Privacy Policy for Tic-Tac-Toe

Last updated: February 3, 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Tic-Tac-Toe mobile application (hereby referred to as "the App"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our App.

2. Developer Information

App Name: Tic-Tac-Toe
Package ID: com.james.tictactoe
Developer: James

3. Information We Collect

Our App does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. However, we use third-party services that may collect information:

3.1 Google AdMob

Our App displays optional rewarded video advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect the following data:

  • Device Information: Device ID, advertising ID
  • Location Data: Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • App Interactions: Ad views, ad clicks, app usage patterns

For more information about how Google AdMob collects and processes data, please review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game progress and statistics
  • In-game currency (coins)
  • Unlocked themes and animations
  • Game settings (sound, haptics, volume)
  • Daily challenge completion status

This data is stored only on your device and is NOT transmitted to any servers or third parties.

4. How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress
  • Remember your preferences
  • Provide a personalized gaming experience

We do not have access to this data, and it remains on your device.

5. Advertising

Our App includes optional rewarded video advertisements powered by Google AdMob:

  • Ads are completely optional - You are never forced to watch ads
  • No banner ads or interstitials - Ads only appear when you choose to watch them for rewards
  • Daily limits - Maximum of 5 rewarded ads per day
  • Cooldown period - 45 seconds between ad views

AdMob may use your data for personalized advertising. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect or transmit personal information, there is no risk of children's data being compromised.

7. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using your device's secure storage mechanisms. We do not transmit this data to any servers. The security of your device storage is managed by your device's operating system.

8. Data Deletion

To delete all locally stored game data:

  • Uninstall the App from your device
  • Or clear the App's data through your device settings

This will permanently delete all game progress, coins, and settings from your device.

9. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying optional rewarded video advertisements

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.

11. No Data Collection or Sharing

To be clear:

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties (except AdMob's automatic collection)
  • We do NOT have user accounts or login systems
  • We do NOT track you across other apps or websites
  • We do NOT have access to your game data

12. Your Consent

By using our App, you consent to this privacy policy and agree to the data practices described herein, including Google AdMob's data collection for advertising purposes.
